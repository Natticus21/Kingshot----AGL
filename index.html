⚔ Teleports, Heals, Zeroing & Resets +
You will get a free Teleport every 12 minutes. Gem cost: first 4,000 / second 8,000 / third 12,000.
Free heals occur hourly.

If your troops are zeroed or close to:
Leave Swordland - Wait 12 minutes - Re-enter fully healed
